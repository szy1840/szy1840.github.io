About

Hi, I'm Ziyan.

I'm a software engineer and founder. I like building products end to end, from the SwiftUI on the screen to the data model underneath, and I care a little too much about the parts users never see.

Z

I started in iOS and kept following the stack downward: concurrency, system design, serverless backends, and the architecture decisions that decide whether a product feels fast or fragile. Most recently I was a founding engineer at Aigeo, and I build side projects and the occasional startup of my own.

What I care about

  • Craft. Small details compound. The drawer that doesn't jitter, the query that doesn't fan out badly: these are the difference between fine and good.
  • Clarity. Whether it's code or an essay, I'm trying to make a complicated thing feel simple without lying about the complexity.
  • Thinking in public. I write to find out what I believe. The notebook on this site is part of that.

Beyond the keyboard

Outside of building things, I read and write, often in Chinese, about technology, learning, and the occasional argument I can't stop having with myself. You'll find that side of me under Notes & Essays.
